       AI Ethics 
          Creating a healthy digital culture with technology and people
         Kakao Algorithm Ethics enhance mankind’s benefit and wellbeing and keep all efforts for algorithm development within the ethical framework of society;  ensure that algorithms shall not generate biased results;  collect and manage data for algorithm learning in accordance with social ethical norms;  ensure that algorithm shall not be manipulated internally or externally;  provide explanations on algorithm to strengthen users’ trust to the extent that it does not compromise corporate competitiveness;  ensure that algorithm-based technology and service embrace our society;  put priority on child and youth not to be exposed to inappropriate information and threat from the stages of algorithm development and service design, and  make our best efforts to protect users' privacy throughout the entire process of designing and operating services and technologies that utilize algorithms.  맨 위로 이동  하단 메뉴            서비스 이용정보 Terms of ServiceOpen a new windowLocation Terms of ServiceOpen a new windowPrivacy PolicyOpen a new windowOperation PolicyOpen a new windowAccessibility InformationOpen a new windowBrand Protection PolicyOpen a new windowNoticeOpen a new windowContact UsOpen a new window
