Growing the artificial intelligence industry in the UK

This independent review, carried out by Professor Dame Wendy Hall and Jérôme Pesenti reports on how the Artificial Intelligence industry can be grown in the UK.

From:
Department for Science, Innovation and Technology, Department for Digital, Culture, Media & Sport and Department for Business, Energy & Industrial Strategy

Details

This independent review, carried out by Dame Wendy Hall FRS FREng (Regius Professor of Computer Science, University of Southampton) and Jérôme Pesenti (CEO, BenevelentTech), reports on how the Artificial Intelligence industry can be grown in the UK.

It makes recommendations around skills, data, research and adoption of AI.
