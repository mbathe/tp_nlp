For Technologists

A world where technology is aligned with humanity’s best interests can’t happen without you.

Take Our Free Online Course

To build technology that matters tomorrow, we have to start with different principles today.

Learn how to create technology that treats attention and intention as sacred, protects well-being, and builds our capacity to address urgent challenges.

Listen to Our Podcast

Join co-hosts Tristan Harris and Aza Raskin as they explore technology’s intersections with the world, from economics to global coordination, democracy, psychology, and more with thought leaders and global change-makers.

Watch The Social Dilemma

Seen by over 100 million people globally, this Emmy-winning Netflix documentary explains how a handful of technology designers control the way billions of us think, act, and live our lives.

Join Our Events

We regularly host conversations with experts and advocates at the intersection of humane design, sustainable business models, technology and society, and more. Learn how to integrate cross-disciplinary designs and thinking into your own products.
