AI Singapore

To anchor deep national capabilities in Artificial Intelligence, thereby creating social and economic impacts, growing local talent, building an AI ecosystem and putting Singapore on the world map.

Launched in May 2017, AI Singapore brings together all Singapore-based research institutions and the vibrant ecosystem of AI start-ups and companies developing AI products to perform use-inspired research, grow knowledge, create tools, and develop the talent to power Singapores AI efforts.

Programmes

  • Research
  • Governance
  • Technology
  • Innovation
  • Products
  • LearnAI

AI Governance

Trust and AI

Supporting research that mitigates the risks of AI by promoting fairness, accountability, transparency, ethics, and safety.

Trustworthy AI

Should we trust AI?

Transformative technology offers tremendous opportunities but raises ethical concerns and the potential for harm. This pillar supports research that mitigates the risks of AI by promoting fairness, accountability, transparency, ethics, and safety. Governance is broadly understood to include laws, markets, networks, standards, and other tools.

Trust in AI

Will we trust AI?

The utilisation of AI will be enhanced by faith that the end-to-end process is robust and accountable. The pillar also supports interdisciplinary research into understanding the factors that shape perceptions of human-machine interaction, influencing the adoption of beneficial AI.

Research Areas

(non-exhaustive)

  • Fairness Ensure that the benefits of AI are properly shared among the community (Bias detection & prevention)
  • Transparency / explainability Ensure that systems appropriately enable those affected to understand the process in general and a specific outcome in particular (Transparency tools; explainability standards & measures)
  • Safety & Security Ensure that AI systems are safe to use and appropriately protected against hacking and data breaches (Safety certification;Data protection & cybersecurity)
  • Accountability Ensure that AI systems are properly regulated minimizing risk and properly allocating losses (Liability and compensation framework)
  • Ethics & human-centricity Ensure that AI serves the interests of the broader community (Values and trust in AI)

AI Singapore

To anchor deep national capabilities in Artificial Intelligence, thereby creating social and economic impacts, growing local talent, building an AI ecosystem and putting Singapore on the world map.

Launched in May 2017, AI Singapore brings together all Singapore-based research institutions and the vibrant ecosystem of AI start-ups and companies developing AI products to perform use-inspired research, grow knowledge, create tools, and develop the talent to power Singapores AI efforts.

Programmes

AI Research

Sustain investments in fundamental AI research to ensure SGs relevance in the global AI race

  • Build strategic capabilities
  • Retain and nurture local scientific talents
  • Preposition Singapore for future applications

AI Governance

Research in governance, ethics and accountability of AI systems at the national level

  • Fairness, accountability and transparency of AI systems
  • Privacy and Ethics
  • Risk and mitigations

AI Technology

Support applied AI research projects which are aligned to the National AI Strategy

  • AI solutions with high social and economic impacts
  • Sector-wide or national-scale challenges

AI Innovation

Catalyse and support more widespread industry adoption of AI

  • AI adoption in industry through talent and projects
  • AI standards
  • Industry Consortia

  • Robotic Process Automation
  • Computer Vision
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Large Language Model
