It respects and protects your civil liberties , reduces inequality , and benefits democracy . Human Effort Technology that respects human effort is functional , convenient , and reliable . It is thoughtful and accommodating ; not arrogant or demanding . It understands that you might be distracted or differently-abled . It respects the limited time you have on this planet . Human Experience Technology that respects human experience is beautiful , magical , and delightful . It just works . It ’ s intuitive . It ’ s invisible . It recedes into the background of your life . It gives you joy . It empowers you with superpowers . It puts a smile on your face and makes your life better .
